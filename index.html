Tournaments

Indian Premier League (IPL)

The IPL is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, contested by franchise teams representing different cities. It is one of the most popular and lucrative cricket leagues globally.

Duration: March - May

Ranji Trophy

The Ranji Trophy is a domestic first-class cricket championship played in India between different regional and state cricket teams.

Duration: November - February

Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy

A Twenty20 cricket domestic championship in India. This competition is named after the famous Indian cricketer Syed Mushtaq Ali and serves as a breeding ground for IPL and international talents.

Duration: January - February

Vijay Hazare Trophy

The Vijay Hazare Trophy is an annual limited-overs cricket domestic competition played in India. It is named after the legendary cricketer Vijay Hazare.

Duration: September - December

Duleep Trophy

The Duleep Trophy is a domestic first-class cricket competition contested between teams representing geographical zones of India. It is a great platform for players to prove their mettle.

Duration: September - October

Deodhar Trophy

The Deodhar Trophy is a one-day cricket competition in Indian domestic cricket, named after Prof. D. B. Deodhar. It is considered the “cricket supreme” cup in India.

Duration: February - March
